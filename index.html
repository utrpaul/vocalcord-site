Vocalcord — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Overview

Vocalcord is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Vocalcord does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data sent to our servers or any third party
  • No access to your contacts, location, camera, or microphone

Local Data

Your selected sound preference and any custom sounds you import are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted off your device.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Vocalcord does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require or request any personal information from any user.

Changes

If we ever update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@vocalcord.app
